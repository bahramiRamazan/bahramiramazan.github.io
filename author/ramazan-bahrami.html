Afghanistan's university Entrance Result

Year 1399 Data-Analysis

About the Data:

  • Afghanistan's Kankor is the governoment universitiese entrance test held accross the country each year and students are accpeted to the different universities based on their performance or score in the test. The data for the year 1399 consists of 158174 rows and 9 columns , the columns are

    ['id', 'name', 'father', 'grandfather', 'school', 'score', 'result','province', 'sex'].

  • The result which is either the name of institute/university department the individual has been accepted or a text saying the individual has not been accepted to any university.The result field also shows if a students has selected from provincial qouta ,which was introduced by MOHE just recently or that the student has participated in general competition. Other than provincial qouta , in some departments there are qouta for girls as well .
