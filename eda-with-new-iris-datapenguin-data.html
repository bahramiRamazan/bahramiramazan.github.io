import pandas as pd

print (penguins.shape)

(344, 17)

The dataset consists of 7 columns.

  1. species: penguin species (Chinstrap, Ad√©lie, or Gentoo)
  2. culmen_length_mm: culmen length (mm)
  3. culmen_depth_mm: culmen depth (mm)
  4. flipper_length_mm: flipper length (mm)
  5. body_mass_g: body mass (g)
  6. island: island name (Dream, Torgersen, or Biscoe) in the Palmer Archipelago (Antarctica)
  7. sex: penguin sex

Q. What are culmen length & depth?

  • The culmen is "the upper ridge of a bird's beak"

Q. What are flippers?

  • Penguins wings are called flippers. They are flat, thin, and broad with a long, tapered shape and a blunt, rounded tip
